Family Jungle Adventure Two Nights Three Days

Experience the Heart of Chitwan

Our Family Jungle Adventure is carefully crafted to bring families closer to nature and to each other. Over the course of three unforgettable days, you will witness the majestic wildlife of Chitwan National Park, learn about local conservation efforts, and enjoy the comfort of our boutique resort.

From thrilling jeep safaris to peaceful canoeing along the Rapti River, every moment is designed to be educational, exciting, and safe for all ages. Join us for a journey that your family will cherish forever.

What to Expect

  • Expert naturalists with deep local knowledge
  • Opportunity to see the Greater one-horned Rhinoceros
  • Breathtaking landscapes of Sal forests and grasslands
  • Small group sizes for an intimate experience
  • High standards of safety and wildlife ethics

Sustainable Tourism

At Badreni Jungle Resort, we believe in responsible exploration. Our safaris are designed to minimize impact on the environment while supporting local conservation efforts and community-managed forests.

Program Itinerary

Day One Arrival and Riverside Sunset

Upon arrival, enjoy a warm welcome and a traditional lunch. In the afternoon, we head to the Rapti River for a serene sunset view, followed by a cultural Tharu dance performance in the evening.

Day Two Deep Jungle Adventure

Start your day with an early morning bird watching tour. After breakfast, embark on a full-day jeep safari into the core area of the national park. Enjoy a packed lunch amidst the wilderness.

Day Three Canoeing and Departure

A peaceful morning canoeing trip down the Rapti River offers sightings of crocodiles and aquatic birds. After a farewell brunch, we assist with your departure from the resort.
